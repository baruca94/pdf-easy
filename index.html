What is inside

Six groups of tools, one single file

Everything most people need to do with a PDF over a year, with no licence and without asking anyone's permission.

01Organise

Merge, split, extract pages, drag to reorder, rotate, crop margins, insert blank sheets, N pages per sheet, resize to A4.

02Convert

PDF to Word, Excel, PowerPoint, images or text. And back: Word, Excel, images or text into PDF, with real margins and page breaks.

03Sign

Draw your signature, upload a photo of it or paste it with Ctrl+V, keep it in your library and drop it anywhere: cover page, last page or inside a table cell.

04Edit

Fix a line of the original text, write on top, cover or genuinely erase an area. Fill forms in and flatten them so nobody can change them.

05Protect

Real redaction of personal data, metadata cleaning, and a check of every file for active content before it opens.

06And more

OCR for scanned documents, visual version comparison, PDF/A archiving, QR codes, measuring on plans with calibration, and batch mode.

Use it on your own machine

Three steps, and it works even with no internet

There is no installer, no account, no extension to approve. It is an HTML file: your browser already knows how to open it.

STEP 01

Download the file

Press “Download for offline use”. You get one single file, PDF-Easy.html, in your downloads folder.

STEP 02

Open it with a double-click

It opens in your browser like any page. Move it to your desktop, a network folder or a USB stick: it carries everything with it.

STEP 03

Work on your PDFs

Have a connection the first time so the libraries download. After that it works offline just the same, and your files still never leave the machine.

No hand-waving

What it does well, and what it cannot do

A browser has real limits. They are written here and inside the tool itself, because knowing where the limit is is what makes the rest trustworthy.

Does it, and does it well

  • ✓Merge, split and reorder with a preview of every page.
  • ✓Compress scans by 40–90%, or lightly while keeping the text intact.
  • ✓Sign with an image and place it precisely, even inside a table.
  • ✓Redact for real: the erased area is rasterised, the data is gone.
  • ✓Tables into Excel with amounts, dates and percentages as values, not text.

Does not do it, and says so

  • ✗Reflowing paragraphs like Word. Editing is line by line with a background patch.
  • ✗A Word file both faithful and editable. You choose: identical copy, or approximate editable text.
  • ✗Qualified or smartcard signing. That belongs to the official desktop app.
  • ✗Certified PDF/A conformance. It builds the right structure; formal validation is a separate step.
  • ✗Word-by-word comparison. It shows where something changed, not which word.
